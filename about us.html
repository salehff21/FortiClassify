About Us

FortiClassify was created to simplify the process of classifying and organizing data. Whether it's personal, business, or any other type of data, our platform makes it easy for users to categorize their information into four key types: Top Secret, Private, Restricted, and Public. This ensures that sensitive data is securely managed and easily accessible when needed.

The classification types:

  • Top Secret Data: Highly sensitive data that requires the highest level of protection.
  • Secret Data: Confidential data that should only be accessible by authorized individuals.
  • Restricted Data: Data that can be shared under specific conditions with controlled access.
  • Public Data: Data that can be freely shared with anyone.
